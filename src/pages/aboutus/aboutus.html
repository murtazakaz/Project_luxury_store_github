About Luxurri



With a 24/7 service on offer, you can rely upon Luxurri Cars to help you any time of the day or night, whether you missed the last bus into work, or you have caught the last train home, the team at Derby's Luxurri Cars can help you. With their computer booking and dispatch system, you can ensure your request for a taxis close-by will not go amiss, as all cars are fitted with GPS tracking and satellite navigation. In addition, you will receive a text once your car or minibus arrives. whatever the day, whatever the time, Luxurri Cars are the on time airport specialists, providing Derby, Derbyshire and the surrounding areas with a reliable and conscientious service.
